[01] EXPERIENCE PHILOSOPHY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

This is not a website. This is a classified robotics lab interface.

The user journey is designed around 3 psychological pillars:

  I.  CONTROLLED REVELATION — Information is revealed through scroll, not presented
      all at once. The user must move through the experience to unlock content.

  II. MECHANICAL WEIGHT — Scrolling feels heavy and intentional. No bouncy easing.
      No playful physics. Lenis delivers cinematic inertia.

 III. TECHNICAL AUTHORITY — Every typographic choice, animation, and data point
      communicates that ZASH is not a startup. This is precision engineering.
